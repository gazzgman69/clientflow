Areas to Audit

1. Product & Tenancy

What is the canonical tenant identifier and where is it set/read per request?

Do all user-owned tables have tenant_id + index?

Any joins without tenant scoping?

Can a user belong to multiple tenants, and if so, how is context switched and audited?

2. Auth & Sessions

Are passwords hashed (bcrypt/Argon2)?

Are sessions/JWTs tied to tenantId?

Are login/logout flows tenant-aware?

Is there email verification, password reset, optional 2FA?

Any rate-limit/lockout policy for brute force?

3. Data Model & Migrations

Is there a single migration pipeline updating shared schema for all tenants?

Is rollback possible?

Do records include created_by/updated_by (user+tenant) for audit?

4. Integrations (Google/Microsoft)

Where are OAuth tokens stored? Are they encrypted and tenant-scoped?

Are PKCE + state nonce + strict redirect URIs used?

Are token refresh/revoke flows implemented?

Are webhooks/subscription renewal jobs present?

How are provider rate limits handled per tenant?

5. Payments (Stripe)

Is subscription state mirrored from Stripe webhooks only?

Are webhook signatures verified and idempotency keys used?

How are seat changes, trials, proration, and failed payments handled?

6. Background Jobs & Webhooks

Do jobs accept tenantId and run idempotently?

How is duplicate processing prevented (locks/queues)?

What is retry/backoff strategy? Dead-letter handling?

7. Files & Storage

Are uploads handled via an adapter (local/S3) with tenant-prefixed keys?

Are signed URLs used?

Is MIME/size validation enforced?

8. Observability & Ops

Do logs include structured fields and request IDs?

Are per-tenant metrics tracked?

Are health/readiness probes defined?

What SLOs/alerts are configured?

9. Security & Compliance

Are secrets only loaded via env/secret manager (no defaults)?

Is tenant isolation enforced via RLS or guaranteed tenant-scoping helpers?

Is there a GDPR path (export, delete, retention)?

Is access to PII logged/audited?

10. Backups & DR

What is the backup schedule and retention?

Are backups encrypted?

Can a single tenant be restored without affecting others?

11. Environments & Release

Are dev/staging/prod clearly separated with distinct credentials/domains?

Are feature flags in place for risky changes?

Is rollback quick and safe?

12. Email Deliverability

Are SPF/DKIM/DMARC set up per-tenant?

Are bounce/complaints handled?

Is per-tenant sending rate limited?

13. Access & Admin

Is there an internal admin view to impersonate a user within their tenant (with audit)?

Is there a tenant off-boarding flow (cancel billing, export data, delete schedule)?

14. Performance & Limits

Are per-tenant rate limits/quotas enforced (API, emails/day, storage)?

Are queries indexed by tenantId?

Are N+1 query risks monitored?
